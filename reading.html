Recommended Reading

A few books to go deeper — free where the text is public-domain.

  • Athanasius — On the Incarnation freeThe classic short treatise on why God became man.ccel.org
  • The Apostolic Fathers (Lightfoot translation) freeClement, Ignatius, Polycarp, the Didache and more, in one free volume.ccel.org
  • Eusebius — Church History freeThe first history of the Church, from the Apostles to Constantine.newadvent.org
  • Alexander Schmemann — For the Life of the WorldA modern classic on the sacraments and the world as gift.svspress.com
  • Vladimir Lossky — The Mystical Theology of the Eastern ChurchThe landmark 20th-century synthesis of Orthodox theology.en.wikipedia.org
  • Kallistos Ware — The Orthodox WayA warm, accessible introduction to the Orthodox vision of God.svspress.com
  • Jaroslav Pelikan — The Emergence of the Catholic Tradition (100–600)Volume 1 of the great history of the development of doctrine.press.uchicago.edu
  • Georges Florovsky — The Eastern Fathers of the Fourth Century freeA free, masterful survey of the Greek Fathers.holytrinitymission.org
