Recommended Reading

A few books to go deeper — free where the text is public-domain.

  • Athanasius — On the Incarnation free
    The classic short treatise on why God became man.
  • The Apostolic Fathers (Lightfoot translation) free
    Clement, Ignatius, Polycarp, the Didache and more, in one free volume.
  • Eusebius — Church History free
    The first history of the Church, from the Apostles to Constantine.
  • Alexander Schmemann — For the Life of the World
    A modern classic on the sacraments and the world as gift.
  • Vladimir Lossky — The Mystical Theology of the Eastern Church
    The landmark 20th-century synthesis of Orthodox theology.
  • Kallistos Ware — The Orthodox Way
    A warm, accessible introduction to the Orthodox vision of God.
  • Jaroslav Pelikan — The Emergence of the Catholic Tradition (100–600)
    Volume 1 of the great history of the development of doctrine.
  • Georges Florovsky — The Eastern Fathers of the Fourth Century free
    A free, masterful survey of the Greek Fathers.
